The Person Behind the Systems

I understand both
the crop and
the code.

My name is Arun Kumar Vadam. I hold a B.Sc. (Hons.) in Horticulture and currently work as a Farm Operations Manager overseeing chrysanthemum and mixed production across multiple farms.

What I noticed while managing real farm operations is that every existing farm software was built by technology people who had never held a harvesting knife, never timed a pinching operation, never tracked the EC in a mist chamber at 6 AM. The systems were generic. The operations were specific.

So I taught myself Google Sheets automation, Apps Script development, and web application design — specifically to build tools that actually match how farms operate. Not how software companies imagine farms operate.

My expertise spans the entire agricultural and horticultural spectrum — from traditional orchard management where a mango tree has been in the ground for 20 years, to precision hydroponics where EC is checked three times a day. I understand both worlds and build systems for both.
